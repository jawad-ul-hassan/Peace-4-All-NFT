How does it work?

We pay artists to create unique pieces of art with a peace theme. These pictures are sold on an NFT marketplace, and 90% of profit from the sales goes to the ICRC. Every NFT can be resold again, even at a higher price point. Each sale generates a further 10% from which we donate again 90%. Basically there is an income stream that will benefit the International Committee of the Red Cross in the long run when these NFTs sell.
